About Us

Welcome to QazaqFashion – a platform created to support and develop local Kazakhstani brands of fashion, culture and crafts. Our mission is to combine tradition and modernity, providing Kazakhstani designers, artisans and manufacturers with a unique opportunity to promote their products on the world stage.

History of Kazakhstani fashion and crafts

Discover the unique history of Kazakhstani culture. Immerse yourself in the world of traditional crafts and fashion inspired by the heritage of the steppes and mountains. Exclusive interviews with masters, photo shoots with elements of modern trends and traditional motifs - all this awaits you!
