О нас

Welcome to QazaqStyle – a platform created to support and develop local Kazakhstani brands of fashion, culture and crafts. Our mission is to combine tradition and modernity, providing Kazakhstani designers, artisans and manufacturers with a unique opportunity to promote their products on the world stage.

TRADITIONAL CLOTHING

Discover the amazing world of Kazakh traditional clothing, which carefully preserves culture and identity. Each element is a story embodied in fabric that preserves the spirit of the steppes and mountains. These outfits harmoniously combine ancient traditions with modern fashion trends.

CREATION OF UNIQUE PRODUCTS

The QazaqFation platform is an opportunity to create something completely unique. We work with the best designers and artisans to present collections that cannot be found anywhere else. Customization is an opportunity to add your own story to each product, creating something truly yours.

ONLINE LESSONS AND MASTER CLASSES

Try yourself as a craftsman! Our online lessons offer a unique opportunity to learn the secrets of Kazakh crafts. Learn to create your own products, from traditional weaving to jewelry making, in our exciting and educational master classes.
